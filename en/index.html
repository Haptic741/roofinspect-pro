The Problem

Building maintenance remains reactive, fragmented, and risky. Roof inspections are manual, time-consuming, and often performed only after damage occurs. ESG reporting is costly, inconsistent, and difficult to verify.

Insurers, asset managers, and investors lack trusted, auditable data on one of the most critical components of any building: the roof.

The Solution

RoofInspect Pro acts as the digital brain of roof assets, converting drone imagery into structured, actionable intelligence in seconds.

AI-Powered Multimodal Analysis

Automatic detection of cracks, broken tiles, corrosion, moisture, thermal bridges, and biological buildup using visual and thermal data.

Blockchain Roof Passport

Each roof receives an immutable digital identity containing inspection history, maintenance events, and compliance records.

Predictive Maintenance Engine

From detection to action: estimated intervention costs for materials, labor, and design are generated automatically.
